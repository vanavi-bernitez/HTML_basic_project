What is F1?

Formula One (F1) is a high-speed, open-wheel motorsport that involves teams competing in
specially designed single-seat racing cars. The sport features some of the world's most
talented drivers, who compete in races on circuits all over the world. F1 cars are highly
advanced machines that feature cutting-edge aerodynamics, powerful engines, and
advanced electronics. The races are contested over a weekend, with practice sessions,
qualifying, and then the race itself. F1 is widely regarded as the pinnacle of motorsport,
and it is known for its speed, glamour, and excitement.
